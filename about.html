About Amazon

Amazon is one of the world’s largest e-commerce platforms, offering a vast range of products including electronics, clothing, books, and household items. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, it started as an online bookstore and quickly expanded into a global marketplace. Amazon provides services like fast delivery, secure payments, and customer reviews, making online shopping convenient and reliable. It also offers features such as Amazon Prime, which includes benefits like free shipping, streaming, and exclusive deals.
